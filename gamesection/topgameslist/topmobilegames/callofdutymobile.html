About Call of Duty: Mobile

Call of Duty: Mobile is a free-to-play first-person shooter developed by TiMi Studio Group and published by Activision for Android and iOS devices. The game brings the iconic Call of Duty franchise to mobile with console-quality graphics, fluid controls, and multiple competitive modes.

Players can enjoy classic multiplayer modes like Team Deathmatch, Domination, Search & Destroy, and Hardpoint across legendary maps including Nuketown, Crash, Crossfire, and Firing Range.

The game also features a full-scale Battle Royale mode with up to 100 players per match, offering intense survival gameplay across a massive open-world map. Players can choose between first-person (FPS) or third-person (TPS) perspectives, with access to vehicles, custom loadouts, and class-based abilities.

Call of Duty: Mobile includes a ranked multiplayer system, seasonal events, and frequent collaborations with other Call of Duty titles such as Modern Warfare and Warzone. Each new season introduces fresh weapons, maps, operators, and battle passes with exclusive content.

With AAA-quality visuals, responsive controls, voice chat, and high-performance optimization, Call of Duty: Mobile offers one of the best and most complete mobile FPS experiences available. Whether you enjoy close-quarters combat, sniping, or tactical team play, COD Mobile delivers action-packed gameplay for every player.
