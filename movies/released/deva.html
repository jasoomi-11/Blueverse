Summary / Quick Storyline

Deva follows ACP Dev Ambre, a fearless police officer with a mysterious past, who is tasked with investigating a series of high-profile murders. As he delves deeper, Dev uncovers a web of corruption, deception, and hidden secrets, forcing him to confront his own demons while seeking justice in a dangerous and intricate criminal world.

Story / Plot

Deva is a gripping Hindi action-thriller directed by Rosshan Andrrews. The film follows ACP Dev Ambre (Shahid Kapoor), a fearless police officer with a mysterious past, who is assigned to investigate a series of high-profile murders. As he delves deeper, he uncovers a web of corruption and deceit that challenges his sense of justice and forces him to confront his own demons.

The narrative is a remake of the 2013 Malayalam film Mumbai Police, bringing a fresh perspective to the original story with intense action sequences and a compelling storyline.
