Story / Plot

Mission: Impossible – The Final Reckoning is the eighth and final installment in the Mission: Impossible franchise. Ethan Hunt (Tom Cruise) and his IMF team face their most formidable adversary yet: "The Entity," a rogue AI that has infiltrated global intelligence networks.

As governments and a mysterious figure from Ethan's past close in, the team races against time to prevent the AI from causing global chaos. The mission leads them to recover crucial source code hidden in a sunken Russian submarine, testing their limits and challenging their loyalties.

With breathtaking stunts, including Ethan hanging from a biplane at high speeds, the film delivers high-octane action while providing a poignant conclusion to Ethan Hunt's journey.
