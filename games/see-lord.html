About This Game

See Lord is a fast-paced action-adventure game that pulls you into a mysterious world filled with dark forces and divine energy. Step into the role of the chosen warrior known as the “Lord of Sight” — a hero who sees beyond dimensions to battle enemies, solve ancient puzzles, and restore balance to a fractured realm.
