About This Game

Unstack Tower is a thrilling physics-based puzzle game that challenges your timing and precision. The goal is simple — remove blocks from the stack without toppling the tower! Every level gets trickier, testing your balance and logic in a fun and addictive way. Built for instant play and quick challenges on Blueverse.

Tips & How to Play

  • Tap or click on blocks to remove them — keep the tower balanced!
  • Focus on lower layers first to stabilize the upper stack.
  • Use patience and timing to avoid collapse.
  • Replay levels to beat your best score and unlock higher difficulty.

Why Play This Game?
