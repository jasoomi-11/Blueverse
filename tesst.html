Story / Plot

Baaghi 4 (2025) continues the saga of Ronnie, a fearless fighter who has dedicated his life to protecting the innocent and confronting corruption. When a covert mission goes wrong overseas, Ronnie finds himself framed for an international crime he didn’t commit, forcing him to fight against a powerful global syndicate.

Hunted by both foreign intelligence and ruthless mercenaries, Ronnie must race against time to clear his name and save his kidnapped ally. As he uncovers a vast conspiracy that threatens national security, his journey takes him from the streets of Mumbai to high-tech cities and war zones across the world.

With the odds stacked against him, Ronnie pushes his limits like never before — combining raw power, tactical brilliance, and unbreakable determination. Along the way, he reunites with Siya, who becomes his emotional anchor in a mission filled with betrayal and sacrifice.

Directed by Ahmed Khan, Baaghi 4 raises the stakes with spectacular stunts, explosive action choreography, and intense drama. Tiger Shroff delivers his most physically demanding performance yet, blending emotion with high-octane energy in a story about loyalty, honor, and redemption.

From breathtaking chase sequences to heart-wrenching confrontations, Baaghi 4 captures the essence of a hero who refuses to surrender — proving that some battles are not fought for glory, but for justice.
