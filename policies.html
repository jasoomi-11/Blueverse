Privacy & Cookie Policy

Effective date: November 8, 2025

Welcome to Blueverse (the “Site”). Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we may collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. What data we collect

Blueverse is a static website. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or address.

  • Technical data: When you visit, your browser automatically sends standard information like your IP address and user-agent to deliver the website properly.
  • Cookies: We use small cookies or local storage only to remember your cookie preferences and to manage embedded content such as YouTube trailers.

2. Cookies and consent

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help the site function or remember your preferences.

Blueverse uses:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to work (for example, remembering your cookie settings).
  • Embedded content cookies — if you play a YouTube video, YouTube may set its own cookies once you accept them.

You can change or withdraw your consent anytime via the Cookie settings link in our footer or by clearing browser cookies.

3. Embedded content (YouTube and others)

Pages on Blueverse may include embedded videos or media from external platforms such as YouTube. These platforms may collect data about you, use cookies, or track your interaction if you choose to play the media. Such activity is governed by the respective provider’s privacy policy.

4. Analytics (future option)

At this time, Blueverse does not use any analytics or tracking systems. If we add a privacy-friendly analytics tool later (e.g., Google Analytics or Cloudflare Insights), we will update this policy and request consent before enabling it.

5. Your rights

Depending on your region (for example, EU, UK, or California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete any personal data we may hold. Since Blueverse currently stores no personal data, these rights generally do not apply. However, you may still contact us for any privacy concern.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to secure this website and any limited data processed through it. Because the site is static and does not store user accounts or databases, the risk of data exposure is minimal.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Blueverse evolves. Any new effective date will be shown at the top of this page.
